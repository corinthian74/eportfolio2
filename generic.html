About me

Entrepreneur, Investor and Philanthropist

John's journey started in early 1998 when he moved to England after graduation from an Italian university. He worked in his early years at a financial services organisation, where he developed his initial skills in the investment arena. Other experiences were in the IT industry, where he evolved as an RVP Sales for Germany and Italy and EMEA head of sales in another tech company. After over a decade of corporate experience, he started several businesses, the last being a real estate business. In his current role, he is the family officer looking after the family's financial interests.

John is also a philanthropist involved with an Italian leukaemia charity and sponsoring several children in Ethiopia via a local charity.

John has an MBA and has started further studies as an MSc data science specialist.
